About

Hi, my name is Marie Buysse. Welcome to my portfolio website. Around the turn of the year 2014 to 2015, I finally had saved up enough money for my own decent camera. Perfect timing, as I was to embark on many travels during 2015 and would get amazing opportunities to practice the art of photography. Pictures accumulated, and soon I needed to organize all of them. So why not do it here, where everyone can enjoy them. Have a look around, and give a shout if you like them! Hugs, Marie.
